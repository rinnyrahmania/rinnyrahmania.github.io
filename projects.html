Complete Project History

  • Project Alpha: Automated Species Classification (2024)

    **Role:** Lead Researcher. **Status:** Completed.
    Developed a machine learning model using drone imagery to automatically classify vulnerable plant species in protected zones, significantly reducing field survey time.

  • Project Beta: Climate Resilience Modeling for Fisheries (2023)

    **Role:** Data Scientist. **Status:** Published.
    Built predictive models to assess the impact of sea temperature rise on local fishing yields and provided policy recommendations for sustainable resource management.

  • Project Gamma: Citizen Science Water Quality Initiative (2022)

    **Role:** Project Manager. **Status:** Ongoing.
    Established a public outreach program and mobile app to collect real-time data on river health using standardized citizen-collected samples.

  • Project Delta: Tropical Forest Carbon Sequestration Study (2021)

    **Role:** Field Biologist. **Status:** Completed.
    A longitudinal study measuring carbon storage capacity of various tree species across different elevations in Borneo.

  • Project Epsilon: Biodiversity Mapping using LiDAR (2020)

    **Role:** GIS Specialist. **Status:** Completed.
    Utilized airborne LiDAR technology to create high-resolution 3D canopy models for assessing ecosystem complexity and diversity indices.
